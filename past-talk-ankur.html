Building a Life of Purpose

Ankur Warikoo

TEDxMDI Gurgaon | March 12, 2024

Talk Summary

In this engaging and insightful TEDxMDI Gurgaon talk, Ankur Warikoo shares his journey from corporate leadership to becoming one of India's most influential content creators. He dives deep into the concept of living a life of purpose, the importance of self-awareness, and how small, consistent actions can create transformative change. This talk is a must-watch for anyone seeking clarity in their personal and professional life.

About the Speaker

Ankur Warikoo is an entrepreneur, content creator, and mentor, known for his candid and practical advice on careers, personal finance, and life. He was the co-founder and former CEO of Nearbuy.com and is a bestselling author of multiple books. Ankur's work has impacted millions across social media platforms, inspiring them to pursue lives of intention and meaning.
