The Art of Decision Making

By Ankur Warikoo | 12 March 2023 | TEDxMDI Gurgaon

Watch the Talk

Talk Summary

In this thought-provoking TEDxMDI Gurgaon talk, entrepreneur and mentor Ankur Warikoo dives deep into the art of decision-making in a world full of uncertainty. He shares personal stories, powerful frameworks, and practical tools to help individuals make better life and career choices. From understanding opportunity cost to embracing failure, Warikoo offers an inspiring roadmap for anyone looking to take control of their future.

About the Speaker

Ankur Warikoo is an entrepreneur, educator, and content creator who has inspired millions through his talks, books, and online courses. Known for his candid storytelling and practical wisdom, Warikoo brings a relatable and actionable approach to personal growth.
