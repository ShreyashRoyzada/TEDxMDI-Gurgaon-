From Passion to Purpose

Ankur Warikoo | TEDxMDI Gurgaon | 15th March 2023

Talk Summary

In this powerful TEDx talk, Ankur Warikoo shares his journey from being an aspiring astronaut to becoming a successful entrepreneur and mentor. He speaks about the importance of finding your "why", embracing failure, and living life with intention rather than expectation. Through relatable anecdotes and practical wisdom, Warikoo inspires the audience to align their passions with their purpose, creating a life that feels both fulfilling and impactful.

About the Speaker

Ankur Warikoo is an Indian entrepreneur, motivational speaker, and angel investor. Former CEO of Nearbuy, he is widely known for his thought-provoking content on career, personal finance, and self-growth. Warikoo's ability to connect with audiences through honesty and humor has made him one of India's most beloved public speakers.
